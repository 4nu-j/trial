How It Started

We put Rekha
on a ringer tee.
We're not sorry.

AHANKARI started with a simple annoyance: everything "vintage Bollywood" being sold as merch felt like it was made by people who'd never actually watched the films.

So we started making our own. Old film posters, matchbox art, and dialogue we grew up hearing — reprinted on henleys, ringer tees, waffle tees and shirts that actually hold up on real streets.

Every piece carries a bit of "dadi's era" and a bit of right now. That's the whole thing.
